Welcome!

My name is Eric Wan, and I'm a 3rd year CS major at Georgia Tech. My threads are People (Human-Computer Interaction) and Media (Information Visualization).

Have a look at my resume!

I'm using this website to show off a little - you can pore through my amazing resume, and if anything catches your eye, you can (probably) find more info on it here below, as well as my progress/current status on each project!

Let's start with the "Wanitor" (I didn't name it) This past summer, I interned at a wonderful startup called Alii Healthcare where I met some of the most wonderful, brilliant, and hard-working people I know. They've developed an iOS app that connects you to licensed ER physicians to diagnose, treat, and prescribe any medical conditions you have - with none of the wait or cost of an ER visit (check them out, they're truly doing healthcare right). I took on the project to build a web app dashboard (from scratch!!) to display information/metrics for our use. Because of HIPAA and all, I don't currently have any pictures or demos of it, but I hope to rig up some dummy data so that everyone can see how cool it is!

Next, we have "Fuber" - the working title for the Android app some friends and I are working on. It's like Uber, but for food! You can sign up to cook whatever food, whenever you want, and vend it out to anyone who wants it! Hungry for more?

Then, "FireHUD" - another title that... could use some work. Some friends and I are building a heads-up-display to be mounted on a firefighter's mask to provide them with information/data that has the potential to save lives. Turn up the heat!
